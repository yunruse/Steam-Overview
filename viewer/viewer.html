Managing games in steam

— To manage game libraries: Steam → Settings → Downloads → Steam Library Folders.
— To find a game in Steam: Mouse over and hit 'Details...'
— To backup, uninstall, verify, browse or move a game: in Steam, Right click → Properties → Local Files.
